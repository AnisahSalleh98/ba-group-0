INTRODUCTION

Behavioural Assessment is designed to measure behavioural competencies that employees must possess if they are to be successful in the workplace and prepared for the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

For a job candidate, behavioural competencies represent behavioural attributes and personality traits whose presence or absence may help to determine his/her level of success in the job for which he/she has applied. These competencies will also be important in the context of career development as an employee progresses in his/her career and takes on jobs that involve more responsibility.

Behavioural Assessment covers eight (8) core behavioural competencies that are important in a Fourth Industrial Revolution workplace:

creativity

Thinking outside the box, transforming ideas into realities.

innovation

Trying new things and introducing improvements.

productivity

Working diligently and producing good results.
